## What is PRMF?

**Post-Retirement Medical Fund (PRMF)** is a fund arrangement designed to help members meet medical costs after retirement by providing comprehensive medical benefits. Planning for post-retirement healthcare is one of the most important financial decisions a person can make — and now, getting clarity on your premiums is easier than ever.

---

## Introducing the PRMF Premium Rate Calculator

The **PRMF Premium Rate Calculator** is a modern, web-based tool that gives members and prospective members **instant, personalized premium quotes** for post-retirement medical coverage.

No more waiting for callbacks. No more manual spreadsheets. Just enter your details and get your premium in seconds.

---

## Key Features

### Instant Premium Calculation
Enter your age, select a benefit option, and choose your family size — your premium is calculated in real time with a single click.

### Four Benefit Options
Choose the coverage level that fits your needs:

| Option     | Coverage Level |
|------------|----------------|
| Option I   | Essential coverage |
| Option II  | Standard coverage |
| Option III | Extended coverage |
| Option IV  | Maximum coverage |

### Flexible Family Coverage
- **M (Principal Only)** — Coverage for the principal member
- **M+1 (Principal + Spouse)** — Coverage for the principal member and one spouse

### Two Payment Structures
The calculator automatically determines the correct payment structure based on age:
- **Annual Premiums** — For active members aged 18–60, paid yearly
- **Lump Sum Payment** — A one-time premium for retirees aged 61–90

### Secure User Accounts
Members can create a free account to:
- Save and track their quote history
- View previous calculations at any time
- Access their personalized dashboard

### Mobile-Friendly Design
The calculator works seamlessly on phones, tablets, and desktops. Members can get a quote from anywhere, at any time.

## How It Works

```
Step 1:  Visit the PRMF Premium Rate Calculator
Step 2:  Sign in or create a free account
Step 3:  Select your age using the interactive slider (18–90)
Step 4:  Choose your benefit option (Option I – IV)
Step 5:  Select your family size (Principal Only or Principal + Spouse)
Step 6:  Click "Calculate Premium" and get your result instantly
```

The result displays:
- Your premium amount in **KES (Kenyan Shillings)**
- The payment type (Annual or Lump Sum)
- A summary of the parameters you selected
- A clear disclaimer about the rate's applicability
